Privacy

Effective 17 July 2026

GIBSON is built to be enjoyed without giving anything up. This page explains, in plain English, what that means.

No accounts, no personal data

GIBSON has no sign-up, no login and no user accounts. We do not collect, store or process your name, email address, or any other personal data.

Your Predictor picks stay on your device

Predictions you make in the Predictor are saved only in your device's local storage so they're still there next time you open the app. They are never uploaded, and we can't see them.

Analytics

We use Vercel Web Analytics to understand how many people visit and which parts of GIBSON get used. It is anonymised and cookie-free — it does not identify you or track you across other sites.

League data

Tables, fixtures and results are fetched from public data providers. Those requests carry no data about you.

Support payments

If you choose to support GIBSON, payment is handled entirely by Ko-fi under Ko-fi's own privacy policy. GIBSON never sees or stores payment details.

No ads, nothing sold

GIBSON shows no adverts, and nothing is sold or shared with anyone.

All ages

Because GIBSON collects no personal data at all, it is suitable for users of all ages.

Contact
